About the Course

Core Information

  • The only prerequisite for the course is basic programming skills and familiarity with databases, e.g. SQL. Having written web application will be helpful and the preferred programming language is Python.
  • Please speak with the instructor if you are concerned about ability to do the course.
  • This is a project oriented course. The course does not have homework assignments or exams. Students work in small teams to develop a cloud based application. Final grade is based on quality and completeness of the project.
  • Project teams will have to submit weekly status reports and meet with the instructor at least once every two weeks.
